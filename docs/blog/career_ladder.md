## 12.23 职业的信任阶梯
> The Career Trust Ladder
From a new grad to a Leader, your career journey is all about gaining trust. It goes like this Can you be trusted to 
>
> 1. complete a task 完成一个任务
> 
> 2. solve a problem 解决一个问题
> 
> 3. solve a problem while partnering well with others 和他人一起解决问题
> 
> 4. figure out the right problems to solve 搞清楚要解决什么问题
> 
> 5. inspire others to solve the right problems 鼓励他人去解决真正的问题

我现在大概是在第三层到第四层的路上。
有能力和他人一起去解决能力，但是对于要解决什么样的问题，还有点模糊。
